Yejin Ha(b. 1997 in Busan, Korea) is an artist/designer based on NYC and Korea. She is visually communicating with various art mediums such as photography, film, drawing, creative writing, and graphic design.

She pursued her BFA in Photography at Pratt Institute with a Minor in Film. Her works focus on identity, East-Asian arts, and intimacy.

She always challenges herself to approach/learn new methods, so that she could fully visualize her concepts.
